Course Overview

This programme covers a broad range of manufacturing systems, including fully automated advanced manufacturing, material transfer performed with conveyors and robots, manufacturing processes without manual alignments, fully integrated gantry system for automatic loading and unloading, flexibility of jigs and fixture selections, and automatic storage and virtual control machines. The specialisation of this programme involves studying about the intertwining of practical automations, electronics and manufacturing processes with flexible manufacturing systems and computer integrated manufacturing.
